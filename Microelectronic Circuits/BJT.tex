$i_C$ 有三種表示法
\begin{align*}
	i_C &= I_s \exp(\frac{V_{BE}}{V_T}) \\
	i_C &= \alpha i_E \\
	i_C &= \beta i_B
\end{align*}
By KCL, we have
\begin{align*}
	i_E &= i_C + i_B = (1 + \beta) i_B \\
	\alpha &= \frac{\beta}{1 + \beta}
\end{align*}
